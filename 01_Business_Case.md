# Business Case

## Problem Statement

The current Virtual Learning Environment (VLE) is outdated, difficult to scale, and provides a poor user experience for students and staff.

## Objectives

* Improve accessibility and usability
* Support hybrid learning models
* Reduce maintenance costs

## Options Considered

1. Do nothing
2. Upgrade existing system 
3. Implement new cloud-based platform (Preferred)

## Benefits

* Improved student satisfaction
* Increased operational efficiency
* Long-term cost savings

## Risks

* Resistance to change
* Implementation delays
* Budget overruns

## Estimated Budget

£1.5M

## Recommendation

Proceed with implementation of a new cloud-based digital learning platform.
