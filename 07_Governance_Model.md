# Governance Model

## Governance Structure

### Steering Committee

* IT Director (Chair)
* Faculty Representatives
* Finance Lead

### Project Team

* Project Manager
* Technical Lead
* Business Analyst
* Vendor Team

## Reporting Cadence

* Weekly project team meetings
* Monthly steering committee meetings

## Decision Making

* Major decisions escalated to Steering Committee
* Day-to-day decisions managed by Project Manager

## Change Control

* All changes logged and assessed
* Approval required for budget/scope changes
